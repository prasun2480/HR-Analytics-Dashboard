# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition using an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in Power BI. The goal is to identify key factors contributing to employee turnover and provide actionable insights for HR teams.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

* **Total Employees:** 1470
* **Attrition Count:** 237
* **Attrition Rate:** 16.1%
* **Average Age:** 36.92
* **Average Salary:** 6.50K
* **Average Years at Company:** 7.01

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 👥 Attrition by Age

* Highest attrition in **26–35 age group**
* Lower attrition in employees aged 55+

### 💰 Attrition by Salary

* Majority attrition occurs in salary slab **Up to 5K**
* Higher salary groups show significantly lower attrition

### 🧑‍💼 Attrition by Job Role

* Laboratory Technician (Highest)
* Sales Executive
* Research Scientist

### 🎓 Attrition by Education

* Life Sciences (38%) contributes most to attrition
* Followed by Medical and Marketing fields

### ⏳ Attrition by Years at Company

* Highest attrition in early years (0–2 years)
* Gradually decreases with experience
